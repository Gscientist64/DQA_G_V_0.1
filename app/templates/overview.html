What is Data Quality Assurance?

Data Quality Assurance (DQA) is a systematic process to ensure the accuracy, completeness, and reliability of healthcare data. It involves regular assessments and validation of data collection, reporting, and management processes.

  • Ensures data accuracy and reliability
  • Identifies and corrects data errors
  • Improves decision-making processes
  • Enhances program performance tracking

Key DQA Components

Data Verification

Cross-checking data against source documents

Validation

Ensuring data meets quality standards

Analysis

Identifying trends and patterns

Improvement

Implementing corrective actions
