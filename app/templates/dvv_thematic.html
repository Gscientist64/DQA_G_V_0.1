About DVV Thematic Analysis

What is DVV?

Data Validation and Verification (DVV) is a tool used to assess the quality of healthcare data by examining accuracy, completeness, and consistency across different thematic areas.

Thematic Areas

The system analyzes indicators across key thematic areas: HTS (Testing), PMTCT (Mother-to-Child), TB Services, HIV Treatment (TX), PrEP, and more.

Components Assessed

Each indicator is scored across four components: Availability, Integrity, Consistency, and Validity to provide a comprehensive quality assessment.
